Please note that this is a family-friendly, non-partisan event. Any unacceptable behavior including but not limited to drunkenness, drug use, harassment, violence, incitement to violence, or other such disruptive activities will not be tolerated and may result in ejection from the Expo.

FAQ

What should I bring?

We recommend bringing with you to the Expo:

  • The MFS Chicago app which can be downloaded here
  • A reusable water bottle
  • Rain coat/poncho (an umbrella is not recommended given crowd size)
  • A bag for holding materials handed out by exhibitors
  • A bag for holding trash (littering will not be permitted)


What happens to the Expo if it rains?

We will continue to hold the Expo if the rain is manageable. If there is a severe storm forecast, we will cancel the Expo and notify you through social media and the MFS Chicago app.


I would like to volunteer at the Expo. Who should I contact?

Please email volunteer@sciencemarchchicago.org and specify that you would be interested in helping at the Expo. Please note that we also have other volunteer opportunities at the rally and march. For more information, visit our volunteer page here.


Who should I contact if I have any other questions?

Please email expo@sciencemarchchicago.org and include “Visitor: “ in the subject line before the title of your email.
