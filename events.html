Astronomy on Tap: Yuri's Night & March for Science Edition

  • Date:  Wednesday, April 12th
  • Time:  7:00 PM - 9:00PM
  • Location:  The Map Room 1949 N Hoyne Ave.
AoT is back! This time in collaboration with March for Science Chicago!!

Join us for a Yuri's Night edition of Chicago Astronomy on Tap hosted by Univ. of Chicago! For the uninitiated, Astronomy on Tap is a multi-city initiative organized to bring astrophysics conversations to a bar near you. With a tagline 'Science is even better with beer!', AoT is about accessible science talks and trivia in a friendly informal environment!
