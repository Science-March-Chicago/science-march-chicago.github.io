Virtual March

Even if you can’t make it to Columbus Dr for the rally, march and expo, we want to make sure you have an opportunity to connect with the event as much as possible. Here are the ways you can be with us from wherever you are!

In general, follow the hashtag #MFSChi on Twitter, including our Twitter Feed, for the most up-to-date posts about the event itself. Tweet with that hashtag to join us in supporting, safeguarding, celebrating and encouraging science!

Listen to the rally and march LIVE! Thanks to 98.3 WRLR for providing a live broadcast of the March for Science Chicago.
